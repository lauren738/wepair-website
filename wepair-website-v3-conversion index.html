Our Story

Built by the Workforce, Championed by Clinicians

WePair Health was founded because we've lived the frustration of fragmented training pathways, unclear career advancement, and lack of practical support for frontline public health workers.

We're a cross-sector team committed to this workforce: public health professionals with deep community roots, clinician champions advocating for workforce equity, Community Health Workers and doulas who know what training really needs to include, and tech innovators building tools that actually work in the field.

Our Mission

Expand frontline career opportunities by enabling individuals with lived experiences to reach their full potential through clear training pathways, support, and community connection.

Our Vision

A healthcare system where every frontline professional thrives, fostering equity, social justice, and community-rooted care.

"We are invested because it's personal. We are driven because it's necessary."

Frequently Asked Questions

What's the difference between CHW Track, Doula Track, and Dual Pathway?
CHW Track: Community Health Worker training aligned with national competency standards. Doula Track: Full Spectrum Doula certification for comprehensive perinatal support. Dual Pathway: Complete both core trainings for dual credentials. You can also add specialty courses.
Can I take a specialty without completing Core training?
Yes, if you have verified prior training or experience. You'll earn a "Specialty Certificate (Accelerated Entry)". If you later complete Core, we'll upgrade your credential to the standard version.
How much does training cost?
Limited Time Launch Special (Ends April 30, 2026): Core training (CHW & Doula) is FREE. Specialty courses are $15 each. After April 30: Core training will be $149, specialties $49 each. Sign up now to lock in free access to any courses you enroll in during the promotional period.
Is creating a profile really free?
Yes! Creating your WePair profile is always 100% free with no credit card required.
Can my employer pay for my training?
Absolutely. We offer employer-sponsored training programs where organizations can cover the cost of Core, Birthworker, and Specialty courses for their teams. Contact us to learn more.
Where is the AI Coach?
The AI Coach is built into your training courses. Once you enroll, you'll have AI support to help you learn.
What resources are available after I complete training?
You'll have access to: WePair Academy Mobile App for continuing education on-the-go, NoteScribe (our smart documentation tool for client visits, billing, and encounter tracking), and Partner Training & Resources for specialized certifications and advanced workshops.
Which specialty is best for me?
It depends on your interests and community needs. Maternal Mental Health pairs well with birthworker training. Behavioral Health and Alzheimer's Care are high-demand. Climate & Environmental Health is emerging. Browse all specialties to learn more.
Do you help with job placement?
Yes! After completing training, you'll have access to our job board where employers post opportunities specifically for WePair-trained professionals.
